Fintech and finance were worlds I hadn’t encountered until my first job in New York City. SEI, a mid-sized investment firm headquartered in Pennsylvania, became my entry point into that space. My studio functioned as SEI’s in-house design department, where I delivered a wide range of high-quality work across multiple seasons and initiatives. That experience shaped my understanding of financial branding and design at scale.
